Swizzl AI

Swizzl AI is an AI-driven platform designed to enhance sales processes by qualifying leads and preparing customers for purchase decisions. It aims to increase conversion rates and reduce appointment times by providing sales teams with insights into customer needs and tailored coaching strategies.

Platform Highlights

The platform integrates seamlessly with existing websites and sales funnels, offering tools suitable for both seasoned and new sales professionals.
