Your Challenge with Today's Car Buyers

The Problem: Indecision and Resistance

Many customers don't know exactly what they want when they begin searching. Your top performers naturally take a consultative approach, but most salespeople struggle to guide customers effectively.


Meanwhile, buyers grow increasingly resistant to sales conversations, and basic chatbots fail to adequately prepare them for meaningful showroom visits.

The Solution: Swizzl

Swizzl helps customers discover their true needs and make informed trade-off decisions before ever stepping foot in your dealership. This means:

  • Better-qualified appointments with buyers who know what they want
  • Sales teams equipped with detailed customer preferences from the start
  • More efficient conversations that lead to faster closings
  • No more wasted time on unprepared shoppers
